3. Configuring Parameters

Tap the Settings button to set your parameters. In the settings interface you can change:

  • Fee (in SOL): fee charged per operation.
  • Tip (in SOL): additional charge per operation.
  • Trading Bot Commission (in %): commission applied only on purchases.
  • Purchase Amount (in SOL): the amount used for simulating each purchase.

After updating, the bot will display your current settings.

4. Loading Wallet Activities

  1. Tap Copy Wallet Simulation.
  2. Enter the wallet address.
  3. Select the period for loading activities (e.g., 1 day, 1 week, 1 month, or specify a custom period).
  4. The bot will load activities using pagination (100 records per request) and filter them by the chosen period.
  5. Once loaded, you will see a message like: ‚úÖ Received 25 activities for the selected period.

5. Simulating Operations

When you tap the Start Simulation button, the bot will:

  • Display a message indicating that the simulation has started.
  • Fully repeat the wallet‚Äôs operations (both buys and sells) in the same order and proportions as the original.
  • Calculate the purchase or sale price for each operation using historical data.
  • The simulation takes into account your configured settings (purchase amount, fees, tip, and commission).
  • Results are calculated for each token, including ROI, PnL, and the number of buy and sell operations.
