User Guide for the Copy Simulation Bot

This guide explains how to use the copy simulation bot via Telegram. The bot allows you to:

  • Load wallet activities.
  • Configure parameters such as fees, purchase amount, tips, etc.
  • Simulate operations by fully replicating the wallet's actions or by applying your own custom strategy.
  • Receive a final simulation report with detailed information for each token.

3. Configuring Parameters

Tap the Settings button to set your parameters. In the settings interface, you can change:

  • Fee (in SOL): a fee charged per operation.
  • Tip (in SOL): an additional charge per operation.
  • Trading Bot Commission (in %): a commission applied on each purchase.
  • Purchase Amount (in SOL): the amount used for simulating each purchase.

After updating, the bot will display your current settings.

5. Loading Wallet Activities

  1. Tap the Copy wallet simulation button.
  2. Enter the wallet address.
  3. Choose the period for loading activities (e.g., 1 day, 1 week, 1 month, or specify a custom period).
  4. The bot will load activities using pagination (100 records per request) and filter them by the selected period.
  5. Once loaded, you will see a message like: ‚úÖ Received 25 deals for the selected period.

6. Simulating Operations

After loading the deals, tap the Start simulation button. The bot will:

  • Calculate the purchase or sale price for each operation using historical data.
  • Take into account your configured settings (purchase amount, fees, tip, and commission).
  • Calculate ROI, PnL, and the number of buys and sells for each token.
